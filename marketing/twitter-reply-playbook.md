# Twitter reply playbook — Statedoku launch

50 niche accounts to engage with **manually** before launch (May 18 → June 1).
Each entry has 2-3 reply angles. Pick the one that fits the actual tweet you see.

**Time budget**: 20 min/day for 10 days. Don't go faster — Twitter's spam detector
flags rapid-fire engagement.

**Rules**:
- Read the tweet first. If the angle doesn't fit, skip — don't force it.
- Replies should be 1-2 sentences. Specific. Human. No "🔥💯".
- If they have <5k followers and you reply thoughtfully, ~40% notice and many follow back.
- Like 1-2 of their other recent tweets while you're there. That's the signal.

## 🎯 What "good engagement" looks like

After 10 days you should have:
- ~10 of the 50 accounts following you back (20% conversion)
- 2-3 mini-conversations where you replied to their reply (real signal to algo)
- 1-2 quote-tweets from accounts you engaged with
- Your last 10 tweets in the niche → algo registers you as "geography puzzle person"

If you have 0 follows back after day 5, your replies are too generic. Be more specific.
If you have 0 conversations going by day 8, you're treating it like marketing instead of community. Slow down.

The goal isn't reach — it's **2-3 real ambassadors** who'll RT your launch tweet on June 1.
