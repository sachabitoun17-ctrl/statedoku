Native peoples

Indigenous peoples have inhabited Kansas for thousands of years before European arrival.

European colonization

French exploration and settlement marked early colonial Kansas.

Statehood

Kansas was admitted to the Union in 1861, and is the 34th state.

Modern era

Today Kansas has a population of 1 to 5 million and ranks #15 of 50 by area. The state economy includes significant oil production, has strong agricultural roots. The capital Topeka and largest city Wichita anchor the modern political and economic life.
