Major cities

Anchorage is by far the largest city (~290k, nearly half the state population) but Juneau is the capital — reachable only by sea or air, no road connects it to the rest of Alaska. Fairbanks is the inland hub. Sitka was the Russian capital.

Geography in a nutshell

Mount Denali (20,310 ft) is the highest peak in North America. The Alaska Range, Brooks Range, and Aleutian volcanic arc dominate the geography. Alaska has more coastline than the rest of the United States combined.
